📄 Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 20, 2026

Welcome to Easy Invoice Maker. Your privacy is important to us. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data.

📱 What Data Do We Collect?

We do NOT collect ANY data. All your invoices, customer information, and business settings are stored ONLY on your device. We have no servers, no databases, and no access to your information.

💾 Where Is My Data Stored?

All data is stored locally on your phone using your browser's localStorage. This means:

  • ✅ Your data never leaves your device
  • ✅ We cannot see your invoices or customer details
  • ✅ We cannot access your business information
  • ✅ You are the only person who can see your data

🌐 Do You Need Internet?

The app works completely offline. Internet is only required for:

  • • Installing the app for the first time
  • • Sharing invoices via WhatsApp (opens WhatsApp)
  • • Exporting/Importing data (optional feature)

🔒 Is My Data Secure?

Yes. Since all data stays on your device, only you have access to it. No one else, including us, can view your invoices or customer information.

📧 Email & WhatsApp Sharing

When you share an invoice via WhatsApp or email, the app opens your WhatsApp or email app with the invoice details. We do not store or transmit any data through our servers.

👶 Children's Privacy

Our app is intended for business use and does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13.

🔄 Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will post the changes here. The "Last Updated" date will reflect when changes were made.
